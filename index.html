South Asians making healthy lifestyle decisions
Risk

People from South Asian communities are upto SIX TIMES more likely to have Type 2 Diabetes than the general population.

Effect

Diabetes can lead to increased risk of serious health complications, including chest pain, heart disease, stroke, high blood pressure and narrowing of blood vessels and nerve damage in limbs.

Solution

Diabetes is a lifestyle disease. CHANGING YOUR LIFESTYLE can reduce the risk of diabetes and even remission.
