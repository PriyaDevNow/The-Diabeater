Helping South Asians make healthy lifestyle decisions
RISK

People from South Asian communities are upto SIX TIMES more likely to have Type 2 Diabetes than the general population.

The main causes are:

    - high carbohyrate and high fat diet,
    - genetic disposition to accummulation of fat tissue,
    - sedentary lifestyle.

EFFECTS

Diabetes can lead to serious health complications including heart disease, chronic kidney disease, nerve damage, and other problems with feet, oral health, vision, hearing, and mental health.

SOLUTION

Learn how to prevent or delay onset of diabetes and its complications and improve your overall health.
