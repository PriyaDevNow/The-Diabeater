Helping South Asians make healthy lifestyle decisions to BEAT DIABETES

RISK

People from South Asian communities are upto SIX TIMES more likely to have Type 2 Diabetes than the general population.

The main causes are:

    - high carbohyrate and high fat diet,
    - genetic disposition to accummulation of fat tissue,
    - sedentary lifestyle.

EFFECTS

Diabetes can lead to serious health complications.

This includes heart disease, chronic kidney disease, nerve damage, and other problems with feet, oral health, vision, hearing, and mental health.

SOLUTION
